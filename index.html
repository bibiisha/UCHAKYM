About us

About UCH AKYM Global Logistics

UCH AKYM is a company registered in accordance with Turkmenistan legislation since October 2018

Our company specializes in freight forwarding and logistics, as well as sourcing and procurement services. Since our establishment, we have successfully partnered with leading international oil and gas companies operating in the region. With a robust global network, we are dedicated to delivering the most competitive rates and innovative solutions tailored to meet the demands of even the most challenging projects.

Core business units

One of our core business units is a provision of freight forwarding and logistics services both within Turkmenistan and globally. Additionally, UCH AKYM is renowned for its expertise in the handling and transportation of sensitive dangerous goods, including Class 1 explosives and Class 7 radioactive materials. Our commitment to safety and efficiency ensures that we meet the highest standards in this critical area of logistics.

The company's core business units include:

  • Multimodal freight forwarding and logistics services
  • General and Hazardous Cargo
  • Sourcing and Procurement
  • Warehousing and Distribution, Packing and Crating
  • Customs Brokerage
  • Project Management and Consultancy

Team of Experts
